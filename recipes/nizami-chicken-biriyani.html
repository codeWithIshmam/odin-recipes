Nizami chicken biriyani

Hyderabadi cuisine is top-notch, especially when it comes to lip-smacking biryanis and chicken dishes. If you’re on the hunt for some delicious Nizami food, we’ve got you covered. Keep reading to learn how to make Nizami Chicken Biryani. It is packed with flavours you won’t forget! This Nizami Chicken Biryani recipe is the perfect recipe for Sunday lunches. It is an aromatic biryani that uses chicken leg pieces as its star ingredient!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of licious chicken tagdi biriyani cut
  • 250 ml of cooking oil
  • 1 cup of yougurt
  • 3 onions
  • 2 litr water
  • 50 ml of milk
  • masala
  • bashmati rice
  • ghost paper
  • ghee
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 3 cloves

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken and pat it dry. To it, add the ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt, cumin powder, cloves, bay leaves, cardamom, green chillies, cinnamon, coriander leaves, lemon juice and mint leaves. Combine this well.
  2. In a wok, deep-fry the onions, remove them from the oil and set them aside.
  3. Add half of these onions to the marinated chicken. Add curd, ghee and some of the oil used for deep-frying.
  4. In a cooker, add water, cinnamon, cardamom, peppercorns, cloves, bay leaves, ginger-garlic paste and ghee. Season it with salt. Bring this to a boil.
  5. Add in the basmati rice and cook it till it's about halfway done. Drain it and keep it aside.
  6. Meanwhile, you can soak some saffron in milk.
  7. Add the marination to a cooking pot. This is the bottom layer of the biryani.
  8. Add a third of the rice on top. Add some deep-fried onions, coriander and mint leaves and green chillies on top.
  9. Repeat this process till you use up your ingredients. Make sure that the top layer is of rice.
  10. Make some dents in the rice using the back of a ladle. Pour over the remaining oil, ghee and saffron milk.
  11. Cover your pot with aluminium foil and place the lid over it. Place a heavy utensil over it for added measure.
  12. Cook this Nizami Chicken on a high flame for 5 minutes. Simmer it for another 15-20 minutes.
  13. Once it's done, turn off the heat and let it sit for 10-15 minutes.
